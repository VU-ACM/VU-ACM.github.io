Association of Computing Machinery

What is ACM?

The ACM (Association for Computing Machinery), founded in 1947, is the largest and oldest international scientific and industrial computer society.

Through its many Special Interest Groups, Professional Chapters, and Student Chapters, the ACM fosters research and communication in a broad range of computing areas.
